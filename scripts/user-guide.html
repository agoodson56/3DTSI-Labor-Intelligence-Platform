User Guide

Everything in the app, explained the easy way.

🤔What is this app?

This app is like a stopwatch for work. When you install devices or pull cable, the app times how long it takes. Then it remembers everything — forever.

Why? Because when the company knows how long work really takes, it can plan better jobs, send the right number of people, and win more work. Every time you use it, the company gets smarter.

It is not a timecard. It does not track your breaks or your day. It only times the task you tell it to time.

📱Put the app on your phone

1
Open 3dtsi-lip.pages.dev in your phone's browser (Safari on iPhone, Chrome on Android).
2
On iPhone: tap the Share button (the square with the arrow), then Add to Home Screen. On Android: tap the ⋮ menu, then Add to Home screen.
3
Done! Now there's a 3D LABOR icon on your phone, just like a regular app.

🔑Make an account and sign in

1
On the sign-in screen, tap Create account (3DTSI staff).
2
Type your name, your work email (it must end with @3dtsi.com), and a password with at least 10 characters.
3
If the app asks for a code, check your email for a 6-digit code and type it in. That proves the email is really yours.
4
Now sign in with your email and password. That's it!
Forgot your password? Tap Forgot password? on the sign-in screen and we'll email you a code to set a new one.

📋Pick your project

1
Find your project in the list, or type part of its name in the search box.
2
Even faster: tap 📷 Scan QR and point your camera at the project's QR code (your PM can print one). The app jumps straight to the right project.
3
Tap the project. Now you're ready to track work.

🔧Track installing devices (cameras, readers, strobes…)

1
Tap Device Installation.
2
Pick the System (like Fire Alarm) and the Device (like Horn Strobe).
3
Set how many people are working with the − and + buttons. If you want, pick their names too (optional).
4
Tap the big ▶ START button. The timer starts running.
5
Go do the work! Going to lunch? Tap ❚❚ Pause, then ▶ Resume when you're back. Paused time does not count.
6
All done? Tap ■ Stop and type how many devices you installed (like 47). Tap Complete.

The app instantly shows your numbers — like how many devices per hour your crew did. That result is saved forever and helps the company estimate the next job.

🧵Track pulling cable

1
Tap Cable Installation.
2
Pick the cable type (like Cat6A).
3
For every reel (spool) you'll use, tap + Add reel and type how many feet are on it when you start (usually 1000). You can have up to 30 reels.
4
Set your crew size, then tap ▶ START.
5
Pull cable! Pause for lunch if you need to.
6
When you stop, the app asks how many feet are left on each reel. Look at the reel marks and type it in. The app does the math: started with 1000, have 420 left = you pulled 580 feet. No calculator needed!

🚪Closed the app by accident? No problem

The timer lives on the server, not on your phone. If your phone dies or you close the app, your timer keeps running. Open the app again, go to ⏱️ My Work, and tap your session to get right back to it.

⏱️See your work history

Tap My Work in the menu. The top shows anything still running. Below that is everything you've finished — with your speed numbers for each one. Try to beat your best!

🔒Keep your account safe

Tap your name (bottom of the menu on a computer, top right on a phone) to open your account page. There you can:

  • Change your password — type your old one, then the new one.
  • Turn on MFA — scan a QR code with an authenticator app. After that, signing in also asks for a 6-digit code from your phone. It's like a second lock on your door.
  • See every time someone signed in to your account, and from where.

🗂️For Project Managers: create projects

Tap ⚙️ Admin in the menu, then pick the Projects tab. There are two easy ways:

1
The Excel form: tap ⬇ Download Project Form, fill out the Answer column in Excel (project number, name, customer, systems…), save it, then tap ⬆ Upload Project and pick your file. The project is created instantly with a QR code.
2
Type it in: use the "New project" boxes on the same page.
The systems you list on the form are the only ones technicians will see on that project — fewer wrong choices in the field. Print the QR code (tap QR next to the project) and tape it up in the job trailer.

📊What do the numbers mean?

  • Man hours = clock time × people. Two people working 3 hours = 6 man hours.
  • Earned hours = what the work should have taken according to our estimates.
  • Productivity = earned ÷ spent. Over 100% is winning — the crew beat the estimate. Under 100% means the work took longer than planned.
  • Confidence on Intelligence pages = how much data we have. More completed sessions = more trustworthy numbers.

⚙️For Admins: run the platform

  • Admin → Users: add people, disable accounts, see who has MFA on. Self-registered staff start as Technicians — promote them here.
  • Admin → Roles: tick or untick what each role is allowed to do.
  • Admin → Catalog: add new systems and devices, and set the estimate hours per unit — that number is what crews are measured against.
  • Admin → Projects: the 🗑 button deletes a project. If it already has recorded work, it's archived instead (hidden from the field, history kept).

🆘Something's not working?

  • App looks old or weird? Close it all the way and open it again — it updates itself.
  • Can't sign in? Use Forgot password? on the sign-in screen.
  • Camera won't scan? Just search for the project by name instead.
  • Still stuck? Ask your PM or an admin.
